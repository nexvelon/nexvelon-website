10 / 10Chapter IX — Questions, Answered

Frequently
asked.

Direct answers to the questions homeowners, condo boards, building managers, general contractors, project consultants, and institutional buyers ask Nexvelon most often.

What does Nexvelon Global do?

+
Nexvelon Global is a Canadian security systems integrator. We design, install, supply, and service security systems across Canada. Services include CCTV camera installation, access control systems, intrusion alarms, fire alarm installation (with ULC-listed panels), structured cabling, maglocks, door hardware, intercoms, network switches, elevator phone line setup, and service contracts. We serve homes, condominiums, commercial buildings, industrial sites, and institutional facilities.

What areas in Canada do you serve?

+
Nexvelon Global is a Canadian security systems integrator with primary operations based in the Greater Toronto Area. Locally we cover Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton, and the entire GTA. Provincially we serve all of Ontario. Nationally we operate across Canada — including Ontario, Quebec, British Columbia, Alberta, Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Newfoundland and Labrador, Prince Edward Island, Northwest Territories, Nunavut, and Yukon.

Do you install ULC-listed fire alarm panels?

+
Yes. We install ULC-listed fire alarm panels and complete fire alarm systems to CAN/ULC-S524, ULC-S536, and ULC-S537 standards. The panels and equipment we install are ULC-listed. Nexvelon is not currently a ULC-listed monitoring central station; our fire alarm installations integrate with established ULC-listed monitoring providers where the client requires monitored service.

Can you handle a single camera, or only large projects?

+
Both. From a single doorbell camera at a residence to thousand-camera deployments across industrial complexes, we apply the same standard of design, installation, programming, commissioning, and documentation. There is no project too small or too large.

What's the difference between Integrated Solutions and Guardian?

+
They are two separate Ontario corporations operating under the Nexvelon Global business name. Nexvelon Integrated Solutions Inc. designs, supplies, installs, programs, and commissions security systems. Nexvelon Guardian Inc. supplies, installs, and monitors monitoring panels and services, and provides service contracts, maintenance, and ongoing system care. Each is independently incorporated; the contracts, obligations, and liabilities of one do not bind the other.

Do you work with general contractors and developers?

+
Yes. We partner directly with general contractors, project managers, electrical contractors, design consultants, and property managers across Canada on commercial buildings, condominium developments, industrial sites, and institutional projects. We integrate into your construction schedule and produce all required permit and commissioning documentation.

What permits are required?

+
Depending on the system type, jurisdiction, and province, you may require municipal permits, ESA (Electrical Safety Authority) filings in Ontario, and equivalent provincial electrical authority filings in other provinces. For fire alarm systems, ULC-S537 verification by a qualified party is required nationally. Nexvelon handles all permit applications, AHJ coordination, and filings as part of our standard process.

How quickly can you respond to a service call?

+
For clients on a Nexvelon Guardian service contract, we provide priority response with same-day attendance for emergency issues and scheduled service within 48 hours for non-urgent items where geographically feasible.

How do I get started?

+
Begin with a consultation. Email inquiries@NexvelonGlobal.com or call 1-855-969-8566. We arrange a site walk-through, conduct a threat assessment, and provide a detailed quote at no cost or obligation.
